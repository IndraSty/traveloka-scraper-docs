# How to Get Link

To get the link you want to scrap, you can follow these steps:

## Link to Scrap List Hotel
<br />
<a href="/listHtl-1.png.png" target="_blank">
  <img src="/listHtl-1.png" alt="First Step" width="500"/>
</a>

Please visit [Traveloka.com](https://www.traveloka.com), then go to the Hotel section. Choose the 
destination and check-in date, fill in according to your wishes, then 
click Search Hotel.

<br />
<a href="/listHtl-2.png.png" target="_blank">
  <img src="/listHtl-2.png" alt="Second Step" width="500"/>
</a>

copy the full url of the page.

## Link to Scrap Detail Hotel
<br />
<a href="/htl-1.png.png" target="_blank">
  <img src="/htl-1.png" alt="First Step" width="500"/>
</a>

Visit [Traveloka.com](https://www.traveloka.com), then go to the Hotel section. Choose the 
destination and check-in date, fill in according to your wishes, then 
click Search Hotel.

<br />
<a href="/htl-2.png.png" target="_blank">
  <img src="/htl-2.png" alt="Second Step" width="500"/>
</a>
Select the hotel you want to scrap, then click on the item, until you are 
directed to the hotel details page.
